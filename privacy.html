Privacy Policy

Overview

Konectd Solutions Company, LLC, operating the Konectd Payments & Payroll brand ("Konectd," "we," "us"), respects your privacy. This policy explains how information is collected and used when you visit konectdpayments.com or submit a request.

Information we collect

We may collect information you choose to provide, including your name, company, email, phone number, employee count, current provider, service interests and message. We may also collect technical information such as browser type, device information, referring page, IP-derived location, pages viewed and campaign parameters.

How we use information

We use information to respond to requests, prepare service comparisons, provide and support services, improve the website, measure marketing performance, protect against abuse, and meet legal or regulatory obligations.

Analytics and cookies

Optional analytics may be used after consent to understand website use and campaign performance. Essential storage may be used for security, preferences and site operation. You can change browser settings or clear local storage to manage these technologies.

Service providers

We may share information with contracted technology, fulfillment, underwriting and implementation partners when necessary to evaluate, implement or support requested services. For white-label or partner programs, information may also be shared with the sponsoring organization as described in the applicable relationship. We do not sell personal information as a standalone business model.

Data retention and security

We retain information as reasonably necessary for business, legal and support purposes. No security method is perfect, but we use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards appropriate to the information handled.

Your choices

You may request access, correction or deletion of personal information, subject to legal and operational requirements, by emailing hello@konectdpayments.com.

Contact
