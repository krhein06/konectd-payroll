What does the Lifetime Price Lock cover?

Your agreed rates and pricing structure stay locked while you remain a client. Your total can still change when employee count, payroll frequency, locations or selected services change.

Can Konectd handle payroll taxes?

Yes. Full-service payroll includes automated payroll tax calculations, payments and filings based on the services selected for your account.

Do you support time tracking and GPS?

Yes. Available time and attendance tools include mobile time entry, GPS options, PTO tracking, scheduling and job-cost reporting.

Can employees access their own information?

Yes. Employees can use self-service tools for pay information, tax documents, direct deposit details and other available workforce functions.
